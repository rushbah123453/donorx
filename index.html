About Us

Organ and blood donation is an issue of major concern today. In most cases, the lack of availability of the same is not the major challenge. The absence of efficient means of directing blood and organs from donors to recipients is the greater hurdle. This is what we aim to achieve. Donor-X is an android application and website based networking platform for organ and blood donors and recipients. A person in need of an organ or particular blood group can check the availability of the same on the app and website. He will be informed of a hospital or blood bank having the required blood group nearest to his given location.

Why Choose Us?

We provide absolutely transparent and reliable connectivity between the donors, recipients and hospitals to overcome the problem of ineffecient organ networking.

Donor

A user registering for the first time needs to Sign Up on the Donor-X Android Application or the Website (www.pictdonorx.com) whichever he may use. If the user wishes to be a donor, he can choose to pledge his organs through our platform.

Recepient

A person in need of blood or an organ, can search for the same as per his requirement on the Donor-X app or website. His request will be directed to the nearest hospital or blood bank meeting his requirement.

Hospital/Blood-Bank

When a hospital or blood bank is in need of a particular blood group, it will notify the users of the same, so that they may donate.
